Bulk Coordinate Conversion

Enter Decimal Degrees, Decimal Minutes Seconds, Decimal Degree Minutes, Universal Transverse Mercator, or Military Grid Reference coordinate sets; one per line. This will automatically convert these coordinates to each of the other ones.

Submit Bugs / Feature Requests at the repo: https://github.com/pjobson/bulk-coordinate-converter

  • US Capitals
  • Sovereign / Disputed States
